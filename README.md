# AI-in-healthcare
This is a repo for AI in Healtchare class for aSSIST sutdents

## Patient Profiles

The `patient_profiles.json` file contains 15 representative patient profiles modeling the typical Korean population. These profiles are designed for educational purposes in healthcare AI applications.

### Profile Features

**Demographics:**
- Age range: 8 to 78 years old (pediatric to elderly)
- Gender: Male, Female, and Non-binary representation
- Geographic coverage: 12 cities across South Korea including Seoul, Busan, Daegu, Incheon, Gwangju, Daejeon, Ulsan, Sejong, and provinces

**Health Conditions (varied severity):**
- Serious: Cardiovascular disease, cancer, COPD, osteoporosis
- Moderate: Diabetes, thyroid disorders, mental health conditions, allergies
- Light: Vision problems, mild back pain, seasonal allergies

**Living Habits:**
- Diet patterns (traditional Korean, modern, vegetarian)
- Exercise routines
- Sleep quality and patterns
- Smoking and alcohol consumption
- Stress levels
- Screen time and digital habits
- Social activities
