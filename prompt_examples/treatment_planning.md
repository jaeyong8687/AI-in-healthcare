# Treatment Planning Prompt Examples

## Example 1: Diabetes Management

**Prompt:**
```
You are an AI healthcare advisor. Create a comprehensive treatment plan for a newly diagnosed Type 2 diabetes patient:

Patient Profile:
- Age: 52
- BMI: 31
- HbA1c: 7.8%
- No other comorbidities
- Sedentary lifestyle

Include:
1. Medication recommendations
2. Lifestyle modifications
3. Monitoring schedule
4. Follow-up plan
```

## Example 2: Hypertension Management

**Prompt:**
```
Design a treatment protocol for a patient with newly diagnosed hypertension:

Patient Information:
- Age: 58
- Blood Pressure: 155/98 mmHg
- Family history of cardiovascular disease
- Non-smoker
- Mild physical activity

Provide evidence-based recommendations for:
- First-line antihypertensive therapy
- Lifestyle interventions
- Target blood pressure goals
- Monitoring frequency
```

## Example 3: Chronic Pain Management

**Prompt:**
```
Develop a multimodal pain management plan for a patient with chronic lower back pain:

Patient Details:
- Age: 45
- Duration of pain: 6 months
- Pain intensity: 7/10
- Previous treatments: NSAIDs with partial relief
- No substance abuse history

Include:
- Pharmacological options
- Non-pharmacological interventions
- Physical therapy recommendations
- Red flags to monitor
```
