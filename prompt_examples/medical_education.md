# Medical Education Prompt Examples

## Example 1: Clinical Case Study

**Prompt:**
```
Create a detailed clinical case study for medical students focusing on acute coronary syndrome:

Include:
1. Patient presentation and history
2. Physical examination findings
3. Diagnostic workup
4. Treatment decisions and rationale
5. Learning objectives
6. Discussion questions

Make the case challenging but appropriate for third-year medical students.
```

## Example 2: Pharmacology Review

**Prompt:**
```
Explain the mechanism of action, indications, contraindications, and side effects of the following drug classes:

1. ACE inhibitors
2. Beta-blockers
3. Statins

Use simple language suitable for first-year medical students, and provide clinical examples for each.
```

## Example 3: Clinical Reasoning Exercise

**Prompt:**
```
You are tutoring a medical student on clinical reasoning. Present a stepwise approach to:

Problem: A 72-year-old patient with confusion and falls

Guide the student through:
1. History taking priorities
2. Physical examination focus
3. Differential diagnosis formation
4. Diagnostic test selection
5. Management priorities

Use the Socratic method to help them develop critical thinking skills.
```

## Example 4: Medical Procedure Explanation

**Prompt:**
```
Explain the following procedure to a medical student who will observe it for the first time:

Procedure: Central venous catheter insertion

Cover:
- Indications and contraindications
- Anatomical landmarks
- Step-by-step procedure
- Potential complications
- Post-procedure care

Include tips for what to observe and learn during the procedure.
```
